Rock Paper Scissors

Instructions:

This game is played in the console. You will have to open it depending on the browser that you are using. But try using CTRL + SHIFT + I
This game has been improved to be played from the console into real time browser.

  1. Choose: Rock, Paper or Scissor
  2. Once chosen it will display score in real time.
  3. Every game is five rounds
  4. After five rounds it will give you the final result and who won!
  5. To play again click the Play Again button

Good luck!
