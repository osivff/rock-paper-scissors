Rock Paper Scissors

Instructions:

This game is played in the console. You will have to open it depending on the browser that you are using. But try using CTRL + SHIFT + I

  1. Open console CTRL + ALT + I
  2. Click Play Game button below
  3. In the prompt window enter rock, paper or scissors.
  4. After 5 times of prompting it will give you the final result and who won!
  5. To play again re-load the page.

Good luck!
